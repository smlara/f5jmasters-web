The contest

F5J Masters takes place every year at Escalona del Prado, Segovia. The F5J class covers electric sailplanes up to 4 metres wingspan, with motor use limited to 30 seconds per flight.

The first edition was in 2011. The 2020 and 2021 editions were cancelled due to the pandemic. The 2026 edition was the XIV.

The event regularly draws pilots from Spain and several European countries, using the GliderLink app for real-time tracking and scoring.
